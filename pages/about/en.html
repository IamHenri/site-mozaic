Objectives

Mozaic allows you to organise your content visually, find and access any resource in one click, and share it in two.

Origin of the Project

Mozaic was born from a simple need: to access one’s bookmarks from anywhere, regardless of the device or location.

The most natural way to provide such a service is through a single web page where everything is presented as icons arranged in folders and subfolders, all accessible with a single click.

Code of Conduct

Users: only an email address is required to sign in;

Their data: all users’ bookmarks remain their sole and exclusive property;

Email: if it is ever used, it will be solely for essential communications related to Mozaic.

This free version will remain free.
